Who We Are

Julia Thorne

I’m a speech-language pathologist turned software engineering student, looking to create software that will benefit everyone with communication disorders. While there are many options out there for speech therapy apps, workbooks, and products, I saw a huge need for functional, easy-to-use materials for adults with speech, voice, and language disorders in my work as an SLP. I helped create Speech Sets to fill that need!

Sheila Wang

I’m a UX/UI designer and Technical Support Engineer currently studying software engineering. I have always been interested in applying my technical skills to a product that would make a difference, and Speech Sets was created when Julia and I met and started talking about the needs in the SLP community. Aside from my work and my studies, my main hobby is caring for my two adorable dogs!
