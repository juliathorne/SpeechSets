Why Speech Sets?

Because sometimes, you don't want to practice your speech with cutesy pictures, or words you don't care about, or the same old workbooks.

Sometimes, you just need a list of words tailored to you.

One in 20 U.S. adults report difficulty communicating (NHIS 2019), yet most speech therapy
materials are geared towards kids. Speech Sets can be used to create simple and
functional materials for adults to practice their speech with dignity.

How to Use This Tool

Enter your set search parameters
  on the "Create a Set" page. You can filter
    words by topic, speech sound, and number of syllables.
      Select how many results you want, and voila! You'll get a
        customized list that you can view online or download as a PDF.
